Ouse Valley Way Marathon

Posted by on April 05, 2025 · 1 min read

The annual Ouse Valley Way Marathon and Half Marathon will be on 21st September 2025 organised by the BRJ Run and Tri club.

The scenic route follows the Ouse Valley from Earith to Eynesbury, running along and near to the river on a mix of grass, gravel and tarmac paths.
