Pavenham

The pretty village of Pavenham is rare in that its 700 strong population actually has a phone box with an phone in it. Alas the phone doesn’t work.

But what it lacks in 20th Century communication it makes up for in pretty limestone cottages, a pub and a site of spcial scientific interest. Stevington Marsh is a marshland fed by water from the limestone ridge and feeding the Great Ouse.
